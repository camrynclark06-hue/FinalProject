Overview

The violin is a versatile instrument that builds discipline, fine-motor skills, and musical sensitivity. This page contains practice tips, resources, and an embedded Tableau viz showing data related to music and education.

Back to top

Practice Tips

  1. Short, focused sessions (20 to 30 minutes)
  2. Slow practice with a metronome
  3. Record and review
  4. Set achievable weekly goals

Repertoire ideas

  • Beginner: Suzuki Book 1 pieces
  • Intermediate: Bach Partitas & simple concertos
  • Advanced: Ysaÿe, Paganini excerpts
